Cyberbullying

The Hidden Dangers of Social Media

While social media offers many benefits, it can also expose teenagers to harmful experiences such as cyberbullying, harassment, and negative content. These challenges can significantly affect a teen’s mental health and overall well-being.

  • Cyberbullying: Hurtful comments, rumors, and online harassment that can lead to anxiety, depression, and even suicidal thoughts.
  • Negative Content: Exposure to harmful images, hate speech, and unrealistic standards that contribute to stress and lowered self-esteem.
  • Social Pressure: Teens may feel pressured to conform to trends or engage in risky behavior to fit in online.

What You Should Know: Research indicates that teens who experience cyberbullying are more likely to struggle.
